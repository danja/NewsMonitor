Top AI Blogs and Websites To Follow in 2023

Zuzanna Sieja 23-05-2023

Share:

When you work in artificial intelligence, you must constantly refresh your knowledge and use the latest AI tools to avoid slipping behind the field. And the best way to keep up is to follow the right resources.

We recently recommended the top AI influencers to follow, but there’s more to life than social media. That’s why, today, we’re turning our attention to top websites that focus squarely on AI. 

Not only will we be highlighting websites that offer cutting-edge insights and news in AI, but we’ll also be featuring sites that provide valuable AI tools for improving your work efficiency.

These brilliant AI blogs are well worth your attention. For clarity, we’ve also categorized each one as follows:

  • Business: for advice and ideas on how to use AI in your company
  • News: for general information, including the latest discoveries and innovations
  • Technical: for developers at any level looking to improve, whether through webinars or AI online courses
  • Tools: for exploring and reviews new AI tools and software (check also our list of AI tools to try in 2023)

Ready? Let’s go.

17 AI Blogs and Websites to Read 

1. MIT News (News)

Many technologists dream of studying at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. And it’s easy to see why. MIT conducts all sorts of groundbreaking research, with the progress and results published straight on its blog.

Better still, you can follow the MIT community’s achievements on their YouTube channel, which is the next best thing to actually studying there.

2. The Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (News)

Keeping things academic: the next blog on the list is from another acclaimed US university. The Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab brings together UC Berkeley researchers from machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

And the blog gives BAIR an outlet to share its findings with the world, helping people like you stay up to speed with the latest goings-on in AI.

3. Analytics Vidhya (Developers)

Analytics Vidhya is the blog for data scientists. Specialists from the field share insights via in-depth articles, which include tips and guides that are as useful for amateurs as they are for professionals.

You’ll also find a variety of training programs and an insightful forum — while the site likes to host the odd hackathon, in case you ever want to sharpen your skills.

4. Towards Data Science (Developers/Business)

Towards Data Science is a Medium publication that helps specialists exchange ideas and expand the general understanding of data science. The site invites independent writers to publish articles (and you can find one on there from our very own Przemek ‘Shemmy’ Majewski).

It’s a solid resource for data scientists at any level. You can see what others in your field are working on while staying up to speed on the most important developments.

5. KDnuggets (Developers)

KDnuggets is a must-read, plain and simple. You’ll find new articles every day, covering the latest developments that programmers need to know. You’ll also see the details of upcoming tutorials, courses, and training, which is always a bonus.

Besides that, there’s also a library of data sets that may prove helpful in your latest projects, not forgetting the range of valuable certificates that you can earn. 

6. Science Daily (News)

The name says it all: Science Daily is an excellent source of the latest discoveries, covering everything from psychology to global warming. Of course, we’re here to learn about AI, and there’s plenty of coverage of that — and if you’re interested in academic research, here’s the blog for you.

7. Great Learning (Developers)

If you’re on the hunt for a knowledge base that also offers upskilling, Great Learning is where it’s at. The site has heaps of free tutorials and courses, often targeted at beginners, including cloud foundations, Python for ML, introduction to R, or data visualization.

8. NVIDIA Blog & NVIDIA Developer (Developers/Business)

NVIDIA is like royalty in the tech sector: the company invented the GPU, which creates interactive graphics on laptops, workstations, mobile devices, notebooks, PCs, and more — and the company shares how it all works across these two sites.

The first covers more general technology topics, including how AI is improving sectors like networking and healthcare. The second is more technical, aimed squarely at developers.

Expect more focused subject matter, covering artificial intelligence, data science, and deep learning. The company also runs a very active forum on the site, giving developers a great place to find an answer to nearly any AI-related question.

9. MarkTechPost (Developers/ News)

MarkTechPost is a California-based website for the latest machine learning, deep learning, and data science research. Besides articles for developers, you’ll find there tutorials and universities research. For those who are more into the industry, there are interviews with people from the AI world. 

It’s a relevant source of information to keep up-to-date.

10. TensorFlow Blog (Developers)

The TensorFlow blog is excellent for any programmer who uses TensorFlow and its comprehensive ecosystem of tools, libraries, and community resources. Each article runs through cutting-edge machine learning solutions, helping developers quickly create and deploy ML-based applications.

Each article sits in one of five categories:

  • TensorFlow Core: covering Python development
  • TensorFlow.js: covering how to build models in JavaScript
  • TensorFlow Lite: covering how to deploy ML models on mobile and IoT devices
  • TensorFlow Extended (TFX): covering how to move models from research to production
  • Community: examining the work of the global TensorFlow community

If you’re interested in TensorFlow, you might be curious about using TFX and Vertex AI pipelines in sentiment analysis. We’ve prepared a whole course on this topic.

11. DeepMind Blog (News/Developers)

If you want development know-how and AI news rolled into one, here’s the resource for you. The DeepMind blog offers all the latest insights from the AI world — while the authors love sharing updates about their research, running through everything from the problems to the solutions, with detailed explanations throughout.

DeepMind also has a podcast, so if you prefer to listen instead of reading (say, on your commute or as you drift off the sleep), you can!

12. Towards AI (Developers)

Towards AI is an information hub covering nearly every artificial intelligence topic imaginable. The site collects musings from authors from across fields, so expect articles on subjects like NLP, machine learning, deep learning, JavaScript, Python, data science, and more.

You’ll find a new piece, if not several, nearly every single day, giving you every reason to add Towards AI to your reading routine.

13. ML CMU Blog (Developers)

Here’s another blog for higher education aficionados — this time, from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh. The site mainly focuses on machine learning, with authors ranging from faculty to Ph.D.’s, even students. But the writer isn’t the focus; the blog’s primary goal is to share original research with the world.

14. There’s An AI For That (Tools)

Are you looking to boost your productivity with the latest AI tools? Look no further than “There’s An AI For That” – the world’s largest AI aggregator. With over 300,000 tools available for over 899 tasks, you’re sure to find what you need. The website features a user-friendly search engine that allows you to easily search for the tool you need or browse by category. The list is updated regularly, ensuring you stay up-to-date with the latest AI advancements. Don’t miss out on the opportunity to enhance your work efficiency with the help of cutting-edge AI technology!

15. AI Scout (AI Tools)

Looking for even more AI tools? Well, here’s another site you might find useful. AI Scout is a comprehensive, free directory of AI tools and applications designed to help users discover and explore the rapidly growing world of artificial intelligence. Covering categories such as writing, digital marketing, programming, and web development, AI Scout is constantly updated to include the latest AI tools. This user-friendly platform makes it easy for users to stay current and find the best tools. It is also much more than a directory- a chatbot called ScoutBud can serve as your personal AI consultant, recommending AI tools for any use case.

16. Ofemwire.com (AI Tools)

If you’re looking to explore the vast landscape of AI tools, you may be overwhelmed by the many options available. Fortunately, you don’t have to navigate this terrain alone. That’s where Ofemwire.com comes in. This website specializes in reviewing and writing about a variety of AI tools, including the use of ChatGPT.
Their team of expert writers and reviewers take the time to thoroughly test and evaluate each tool, providing readers with detailed insights into its features, functionalities, and performance. With their comprehensive and unbiased reviews, you can decide which AI tools best suit your needs.
But that’s not all – Ofemwire.com also features articles, news, and opinion pieces related to AI. With their finger on the pulse of the latest developments in the field, they provide a valuable resource for staying up-to-date and informed. Save time and make informed decisions with the help of Ofemwire.com.

17. DLabs.AI (Developers/Business)

We’d be doing ourselves a disservice to forget our blog. After all, we’re a leading Central European software development and consulting company that designs and builds automated, custom AI software solutions for businesses. 

And we have plenty of insights to share. Our AI blog contains tech articles written by specialists in JavaScript, Python, Data Science, Machine Learning, and more.

Plus, we share many practical walkthroughs of how businesses can implement AI and exciting news and titbits from the Artificial Intelligence world.

And if you want to stay up to date with the latest AI news, we recommend you subscribe to our AI newsletter to get a rich source of inspiring ideas, and interesting trends dropped into your inbox every month.
